AuroraBuds Pro — Official Spec Sheet (Lumen Audio)

Overview
AuroraBuds Pro are Lumen Audio's flagship wireless noise-cancelling earbuds, built around personalized sound. Each bud weighs 4.8 grams; the charging case weighs 38 grams.

Sound
- 11 mm titanium-coated dynamic driver per bud.
- Adaptive Sound Map: a 10-second in-app ear scan measures the shape of your ear canal and tunes the EQ specifically for you. No two profiles are the same.
- Custom EQ and touch controls are configured in the Lumen app.

Noise control
- QuietCore 2 hybrid active noise cancellation cancels up to 42 dB of ambient noise.
- Transparency mode lets outside sound in for conversations.

Performance
- Lumen L1 chip. Game Mode drops audio latency to 38 ms.
- Bluetooth 5.3 with multipoint: connect to 2 devices at once.
- 3 microphones per bud with wind-noise reduction for clear calls.

Battery and charging
- 9 hours of playback per charge (7 hours with ANC on).
- 32 hours total with the charging case.
- Fast charge: 10 minutes gives 2 hours of playback.
- Charges over USB-C or Qi wireless.

Durability
- Buds are IPX5 rated (sweat and splash resistant). The case is not water rated.
- Not suitable for swimming or submersion.

Colors: Midnight Black, Cloud White, Sage Green.
In the box: earbuds, charging case, 3 ear-tip sizes (S/M/L), USB-C cable, quick-start guide.
