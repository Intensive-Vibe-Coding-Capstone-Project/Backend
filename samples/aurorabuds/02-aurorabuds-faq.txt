AuroraBuds Pro — Buyer FAQ (Lumen Audio)

Warranty
AuroraBuds Pro come with an 18-month limited warranty. Register your purchase in the Lumen app within 30 days to activate it.

Returns
We offer 14-day free returns. Items must be unused and in the original packaging.

Will they fit small ears?
Yes. They ship with three ear-tip sizes (S/M/L) and each bud is only 4.8 grams. The Adaptive Sound Map scan also tunes the EQ to your ear, so the fit feels balanced.

Are they good for the gym or running?
Yes. The buds are IPX5 sweat and splash resistant with a secure fit. They are not for swimming or submersion.

How is call quality?
Each bud has 3 microphones with wind-noise reduction, so your voice stays clear on calls outdoors.

Do they work with both iPhone and Android?
Yes. They connect over Bluetooth 5.3 on any phone. Full features — Adaptive Sound Map, custom EQ, and touch-control mapping — work through the free Lumen app on both iOS and Android.

Is there a volume safety limit?
Yes. You can turn on a volume-limit option in the Lumen app to protect your hearing.

What if I lose one earbud?
You can buy a single replacement bud through the Lumen app and pair it to your existing case.
